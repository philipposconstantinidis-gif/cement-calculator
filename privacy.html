Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy explains how BasCalc ("we", "our", or "the site") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit cement-calculator.com.

Information We Collect

BasCalc does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use the calculator. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal details unless you voluntarily contact us via the contact page.

However, like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit:

  • Your IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on them
  • The referring website that brought you to us

This information is used solely for analytics — to understand how the site is being used and to improve it over time. It is not used to identify individual users.

Cookies

BasCalc uses cookies for two purposes:

  • Analytics cookies — to measure site usage anonymously
  • Advertising cookies — to deliver relevant ads via Google AdSense

You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer. Disabling cookies will not affect the functionality of the calculator itself.

Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, please review Google's Privacy & Terms.

Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Cloudflare Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. These services collect aggregate, non-personally identifiable information about site usage. None of this information is sold or shared with other parties.

Data Storage and Security

BasCalc does not store any personal data on its servers. All inputs you enter into the calculator (dimensions, weights, etc.) are processed in your browser only and are not transmitted to us. Once you close the page, that information is gone.

The site is hosted on Cloudflare's secure infrastructure and uses HTTPS encryption for all connections.

Your Rights

If you are a UK or EU resident, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • The right to access any personal data we may hold about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of that data
  • The right to object to data processing
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Since we don't collect personal data through the calculator itself, these rights mainly apply if you've contacted us directly. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the contact page.

Children's Privacy

BasCalc is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top.
