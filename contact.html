What to Get in Touch About

We try to respond to all messages within a few working days. Common reasons people contact us:

  • Feedback on the calculator — anything you'd find useful that we don't currently include
  • Reporting errors — if a calculation looks wrong, please let us know with the inputs you used
  • Content suggestions — additional FAQs, guides, or topics you'd like covered
  • Pricing updates — if you're a builders' merchant and want to flag local pricing changes
  • Partnerships — if you run a related construction or DIY site

What We Can't Help With

We're a small DIY tool — we can't provide personalised structural engineering advice, planning permission guidance, or specific recommendations for your project. For these, please consult a qualified professional in your area.

Response Time

We aim to reply to every email within 3-5 working days. If your question is time-sensitive (for example, you're about to start a project this weekend), please mention that in your subject line.
